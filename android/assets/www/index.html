Troubleshooting Section:

Q."Why does my device not switch on or off".

A. If the device does not activate or deactivate there may be a problem with your pronunciation of the object,check the voice input field that shows the text that you have pronounced,if it does not match the device exactly it will not trigger the device.

Q."The application does not pick up on my voice correctly".
A.This may be for several reasons.Make sure you are in a noise free environment ,some background noise may be interfering with your voice input.If you are using your inbuilt computer or headset microphone make sure you are approximately one inch from the microphone and that any sound generating applications that may be running are muted.
